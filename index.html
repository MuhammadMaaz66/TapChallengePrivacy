Privacy Policy for Tap Challenge

Effective date: June 2, 2025

Tap Challenge (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Local Storage: We store your game data such as high scores locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Advertising: We show ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device and usage data to personalize ads. For details, see Google Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

We do not collect or transmit any personal information from users.

Who Can Use This App

Anyone can use Tap Challenge. No personal data is required to play.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your game data at any time by clearing the app storage on your device.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device or Google account settings.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect local data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updated policies will be posted in the app.
